DataLab

DataLab provides a test bed to exercise and qualify geospatial data.

For consumers of data the ability to freely relate data in context with other data sources by location and time provides an opportunity to qualify the value and quality of data for their purposes plus the ability to explore relationships between data sets ahead of potential framework adoption.

Visualise

A web based data visualisation and collaboration environment, DataLab is a test-bed that brings together geospatial data for inspection and interrogation.

Arrange

DataLab allows users to visualise geographical time-based data with an easy-to-use mapping web application. Arrange data in layers, apply filters and aggregrate in combination with other layers.

Understand

DataLab helps to assess the quality of your data and to broadly understand relationships between datasets.
